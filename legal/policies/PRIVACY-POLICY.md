## 1. Introduction

Blaze Sports Intelligence ("we," "our," or "us") operates blazesportsintel.com (the "Platform"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our Platform.

**Your privacy is critically important to us.** We are committed to transparency about our data practices and your rights.

### Key Points

- We collect minimal personal information
- We never sell your personal data
- You have control over your data
- We comply with CCPA, GDPR, and COPPA
- We use cookies for analytics and performance

## 5. Data Retention

### 5.1 Active Accounts

**While your account is active:**
- Account information: Retained until deletion
- Usage data: Retained for 24 months
- IP addresses: Anonymized after 30 days
- Error logs: Retained for 90 days

### 5.2 Deleted Accounts

**When you delete your account:**
- Personal information: Deleted within 30 days

- Backup copies: Purged within 90 days

**Accounts inactive for 3+ years:**
- Notification sent to registered email
- 90-day grace period before deletion

## 6. Your Privacy Rights

### 6.1 All Users

**You have the right to:**

1. **Access:** Request a copy of your personal data
2. **Correction:** Update inaccurate information
3. **Deletion:** Request account and data deletion
4. **Objection:** Opt-out of marketing communications
5. **Portability:** Download your data in machine-readable format

**How to exercise rights:** Email privacy@blazesportsintel.com

### 6.2 California Residents (CCPA)

**Additional rights under CCPA:**

- **Right to Know:** Categories of data collected, sources, and sharing practices
- **Right to Delete:** Request deletion of personal information
- **Right to Opt-Out:** Opt-out of sale of personal data (we don't sell data)
- **Right to Non-Discrimination:** Equal service regardless of rights exercise

**Shine the Light Law:** Once per year, request list of third parties who received personal information for direct marketing (we don't share for this purpose).

**How to submit CCPA requests:**
- Email: privacy@blazesportsintel.com
- Subject line: "CCPA Request - [Access/Delete/Opt-Out]"
- Response time: 45 days (extendable to 90 days with notice)

### 6.3 European Union Residents (GDPR)

**Additional rights under GDPR:**

- **Right to Erasure:** "Right to be forgotten"
- **Right to Restrict Processing:** Limit how we use your data
- **Right to Object:** Object to processing based on legitimate interests
- **Right to Withdraw Consent:** Withdraw consent at any time
- **Right to Lodge Complaint:** File complaint with supervisory authority

**Our EU Representative:** (To be appointed if we process significant EU data)

**How to submit GDPR requests:**
- Email: gdpr@blazesportsintel.com
- Response time: 30 days (extendable to 60 days with notice)

### 6.4 Automated Decision-Making

**We do not use personal data for automated decision-making that produces legal effects or significantly affects you.**

Predictive analytics (e.g., game predictions) use only publicly available sports statistics, not personal data.

## 7. Data Security

### 7.1 Technical Safeguards

**Encryption:**
- TLS 1.3 encryption for all data in transit
- AES-256 encryption for data at rest
- Encrypted database backups

**Access Controls:**
- Role-based access control (RBAC)
- Multi-factor authentication for administrators
- Regular access audits
- Principle of least privilege

**Infrastructure Security:**
- DDoS protection via Cloudflare
- Web Application Firewall (WAF)
- Regular security scans and penetration testing
- Automated vulnerability monitoring

### 7.2 Organizational Safeguards

- Employee background checks
- Data protection training
- Confidentiality agreements
- Incident response plan
- Annual security audits

### 7.3 Breach Notification

**In the event of a data breach:**

1. **Assessment:** Immediate investigation and containment
2. **Notification:** Affected users notified within 72 hours
3. **Remediation:** Steps taken to prevent future breaches
4. **Reporting:** Regulatory notifications as required by law

**Notification includes:**
- Nature of the breach
- Data types affected
- Steps we're taking
- Steps you should take
- Contact information for questions

## 9. Cookie Policy

### 9.1 What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device that help us provide and improve the Platform.

### 9.2 Types of Cookies We Use

**Essential Cookies** (Always Active):
- Authentication and session management
- Security features
- User preferences (theme, timezone)
- Load balancing

**Analytics Cookies** (Opt-In):
- Google Analytics (anonymized IP, usage patterns)
- Cloudflare Analytics (performance metrics)
- Error tracking (Sentry)

**Performance Cookies** (Opt-In):
- CDN caching
- Resource optimization
- A/B testing

### 9.3 Third-Party Cookies

| Provider | Purpose | Privacy Policy |
|----------|---------|----------------|
| Google Analytics | Usage analytics | [Google Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy) |
| Cloudflare | Security and performance | [Cloudflare Privacy Policy](https://www.cloudflare.com/privacypolicy/) |

### 9.4 Cookie Management

**Browser Controls:**
- Chrome: Settings > Privacy > Cookies
- Firefox: Settings > Privacy > Cookies
- Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies
- Edge: Settings > Privacy > Cookies

**Our Cookie Banner:**
- Customize preferences on first visit
- Change preferences anytime via footer link
- Granular control (essential vs. analytics vs. performance)

**Opt-Out Tools:**
- Google Analytics Opt-Out: [Browser Add-On](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout)
- Do Not Track: We honor DNT browser signals

### 9.5 Cookie Lifespan

- Session cookies: Deleted when browser closes
- Persistent cookies: Expire after 12 months
- Authentication tokens: Expire after 30 days inactivity
