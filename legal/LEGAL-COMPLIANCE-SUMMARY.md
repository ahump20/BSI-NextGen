## Key Features

### Privacy Policy Highlights

**Compliance:**
- GDPR Article 5 principles (lawfulness, fairness, transparency)
- CCPA Section 1798.100 (consumer rights)
- COPPA Section 312.4 (parental consent)

**User Rights Implemented:**
- Right to access (data export)
- Right to deletion ("right to be forgotten")
- Right to portability (machine-readable export)
- Right to opt-out (marketing, analytics)
- Right to rectification (data correction)

**Transparency:**
- Data inventory tables (what we collect, why, how long)
- Third-party service disclosures
- Cookie audit log
- Plain language explanations

### Terms of Service Highlights

**Disclaimers:**
- ⚠️ **Gambling Disclaimer:** "THIS PLATFORM IS NOT A GAMBLING SERVICE. Analytics are for informational purposes only. If you have a gambling problem: 1-800-522-4700."
- ⚠️ **Predictive Analytics:** "Predictions do not guarantee outcomes. Do not rely on predictions for financial decisions."
- ⚠️ **Data Accuracy:** "Verify critical information with official sources (MLB.com, NFL.com, NCAA.org)."
- ⚠️ **NIL Valuations (future):** "Estimates only. Not financial advice. Consult professionals before making NIL decisions."

**Arbitration:**
- Binding arbitration for disputes (AAA rules)
- 30-day opt-out period (email legal@blazesportsintel.com)
- Class action waiver
- Small claims court exception (<$10,000)

**Intellectual Property:**
- Platform copyright owned by Blaze Sports Intelligence
- User-generated content license (future)
- Third-party data attribution requirements
- DMCA safe harbor protection

### Cookie Policy Highlights

**Cookie Categories:**
1. **Essential** (always active): Authentication, security, preferences
2. **Analytics** (opt-in): Google Analytics, Cloudflare Analytics, Sentry
3. **Performance** (opt-in): CDN caching, A/B testing

**Consent Management:**
- Granular consent options
- Do Not Track (DNT) support
- Cookie preferences editable anytime
- 12-month consent validity

**Compliance:**
- ePrivacy Directive 2002/58/EC (EU Cookie Law)
- GDPR Article 6 (legal basis for processing)
- CCPA Section 1798.140 (personal information definition)

### DMCA Policy Highlights

**Safe Harbor Protection:**
- Designated DMCA agent (Austin Humphrey)
- Agent registered with U.S. Copyright Office
- Email: dmca@blazesportsintel.com

**Procedures:**
- Takedown notice process (5-7 business day response)
- Counter-notification process (10-14 business day restoration)
- Repeat infringer policy (3 strikes = account termination)

**Third-Party Data:**
- SportsDataIO: Commercial license, no redistribution
- MLB Stats API: Attribution required
- ESPN API: Personal use only
- Team logos: Fair use (news/commentary)

## Risk Assessment

### High Priority Risks (Address Immediately)

1. **No Privacy Policy = GDPR/CCPA Violations**
   - **Risk:** Fines up to €20M or 4% global revenue (GDPR), $7,500 per violation (CCPA)
   - **Mitigation:** Publish Privacy Policy before collecting any user data
   - **Status:** ✅ Policy complete, pending attorney review

2. **No Cookie Consent = ePrivacy Directive Violations**
   - **Risk:** Regulatory action in EU, loss of user trust
   - **Mitigation:** Deploy cookie consent banner on all pages
   - **Status:** ✅ Banner complete, pending deployment

3. **No DMCA Agent = Copyright Liability**
   - **Risk:** Liable for user-uploaded copyright infringement, no safe harbor
   - **Mitigation:** Register DMCA agent with U.S. Copyright Office
   - **Status:** ⏳ Policy complete, registration pending

4. **Inadequate Disclaimers = Liability for User Reliance**
   - **Risk:** Lawsuits if users rely on inaccurate predictions or data
   - **Mitigation:** Prominent disclaimers in Terms and on analytics pages
   - **Status:** ✅ Disclaimers in Terms, need UI implementation

### Medium Priority Risks (Address Within 3 Months)

1. **No Trademark Protection = Brand Infringement**
   - **Risk:** Others can use "Blaze Sports Intelligence" name
   - **Mitigation:** File federal trademark application (USPTO)
   - **Status:** ⏳ Pending trademark search and application

2. **No Privacy Rights System = GDPR/CCPA Non-Compliance**
   - **Risk:** Cannot fulfill user deletion/export requests within legal timelines
   - **Mitigation:** Build data export and deletion APIs
   - **Status:** ⏳ Pending engineering implementation

3. **No Security Audit = Unknown Vulnerabilities**
   - **Risk:** Data breach, regulatory fines, reputational damage
   - **Mitigation:** Annual penetration testing and vulnerability scanning
   - **Status:** ⏳ Pending vendor selection

### Low Priority Risks (Monitor and Address as Needed)

1. **Accessibility Issues = ADA/Section 508 Violations**
   - **Risk:** Lawsuits under ADA Title III (discrimination)
   - **Mitigation:** WCAG 2.1 AA compliance, ongoing audits
   - **Status:** ⏳ Pending initial audit

2. **No Cyber Insurance = Financial Exposure**
   - **Risk:** Out-of-pocket costs for data breach ($50K - $500K+)
   - **Mitigation:** Obtain cyber liability insurance
   - **Status:** ⏳ Pending budget allocation

3. **International Data Transfers = GDPR Article 44 Violations**
   - **Risk:** Regulatory action if EU user data transferred without safeguards
   - **Mitigation:** Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) with service providers
   - **Status:** ✅ Covered in DPAs, pending vendor signatures
