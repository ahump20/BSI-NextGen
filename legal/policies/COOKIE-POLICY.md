## 2. What Are Cookies?

### 2.1 Definition

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website. Cookies allow the website to recognize your device and remember information about your visit.

### 2.2 How Cookies Work

1. **First-Party Cookies:** Set by blazesportsintel.com directly
2. **Third-Party Cookies:** Set by external services (e.g., Google Analytics, Cloudflare)

### 2.3 Cookie Types by Duration

| Type | Description | Lifespan | Example |
|------|-------------|----------|---------|
| **Session Cookies** | Deleted when browser closes | Until session ends | Authentication tokens |
| **Persistent Cookies** | Remain on device for set period | Days to years | User preferences, "Remember Me" |

### 2.4 Similar Technologies

We also use similar tracking technologies:

- **Local Storage:** Browser-based storage for larger data sets
- **Session Storage:** Temporary storage cleared when tab closes
- **Web Beacons:** Invisible images that track page views
- **ETags:** HTTP headers used for caching

## 3. Why We Use Cookies

### 3.1 Essential Operations

**Strictly Necessary Cookies** enable core Platform functionality:

- **Authentication:** Keep you logged in between pages
- **Security:** Protect against cross-site request forgery (CSRF)
- **Load Balancing:** Distribute traffic efficiently
- **Preferences:** Remember your settings (theme, timezone)

**These cookies cannot be disabled** as they are essential for Platform operation.

### 3.2 Performance and Analytics

**Analytics Cookies** help us understand Platform usage:

- **Usage Patterns:** Which features are most popular
- **User Journeys:** How users navigate the Platform
- **Technical Issues:** Where errors occur
- **Performance Metrics:** Page load times, responsiveness

**These cookies require your consent** and can be disabled.

### 3.3 Functionality Enhancement

**Functional Cookies** improve your experience:

- **Personalization:** Remember your favorite teams
- **Content Preferences:** Show relevant sports first
- **Video Playback:** Remember volume and quality settings
- **Chat Support:** Maintain conversation context (future)

**These cookies require your consent** and can be disabled.

### 3.4 Advertising (Future)

**We do not currently use advertising cookies.**

If we introduce advertising in the future:
- You will be notified in advance
- Explicit consent will be required
- Opt-out will be available
- This policy will be updated

## 4. Cookies We Use

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Provider | Duration | Data Stored |
|-------------|---------|----------|----------|-------------|
| `_ga` | Distinguish unique users | Google Analytics | 24 months | Anonymous client ID |
| `_ga_<container-id>` | Session tracking | Google Analytics | 24 months | Session data |
| `_gid` | Distinguish users (24-hour window) | Google Analytics | 24 hours | Anonymous client ID |
| `_gat` | Throttle request rate | Google Analytics | 1 minute | Rate limit data |
| `cf_analytics` | Cloudflare Web Analytics | Cloudflare | Session | Anonymous usage data |
| `sentry_session` | Error tracking | Sentry | Session | Error context (no personal data) |

**Google Analytics Configuration:**
- IP addresses anonymized (`anonymizeIP: true`)
- User-ID tracking disabled
- Demographic and interest reports disabled
- Data sharing with Google disabled

**Opt-out:** [Google Analytics Opt-Out Browser Add-On](https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout)

## 5. Cookie Consent Management

### 5.1 Initial Consent

**When you first visit the Platform:**

1. Cookie consent banner appears
2. You can:
   - **Accept All:** Enable all cookies (essential, analytics, performance)
   - **Reject Non-Essential:** Essential cookies only
   - **Customize:** Granular control over cookie categories

**Consent is recorded and valid for 12 months.**

### 5.2 Changing Your Preferences

**You can update cookie preferences anytime:**

- **Footer Link:** "Cookie Settings" in page footer
- **Account Settings:** Account > Privacy > Cookie Preferences
- **Banner Re-trigger:** Delete `bsi_cookie_consent` cookie to see banner again

### 5.3 Consent Storage

Your cookie preferences are stored in:

- **Cookie:** `bsi_cookie_consent` (12 months)
- **Local Storage:** `cookieConsent` (backup)

**Data stored:**
- Consent status (accepted/rejected)
- Category preferences (essential/analytics/performance)
- Timestamp of consent
- Policy version accepted

## 6. How to Control Cookies

### 6.1 Browser Settings

**All major browsers allow cookie control:**

#### Google Chrome
1. Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies and other site data
2. Options:
   - Block all cookies
   - Block third-party cookies
   - Clear cookies on exit
   - Manage site-specific exceptions

**Chrome Help:** [Manage Cookies in Chrome](https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/95647)

#### Mozilla Firefox
1. Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
2. Options:
   - Block all cookies
   - Block third-party cookies
   - Delete cookies on exit
   - Manage exceptions

**Firefox Help:** [Cookies and Site Data](https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/cookies-information-websites-store-on-your-computer)

#### Safari
1. Preferences > Privacy > Cookies and website data
2. Options:
   - Block all cookies
   - Block third-party cookies
   - Intelligent Tracking Prevention (ITP)

**Safari Help:** [Manage Cookies in Safari](https://support.apple.com/guide/safari/manage-cookies-sfri11471/mac)

#### Microsoft Edge
1. Settings > Cookies and site permissions > Cookies and site data
2. Options:
   - Block all cookies
   - Block third-party cookies
   - Clear cookies on exit

**Edge Help:** [Delete Cookies in Microsoft Edge](https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-edge/delete-cookies-in-microsoft-edge-63947406-40ac-c3b8-57b9-2a946a29ae09)

### 6.2 Mobile Browsers

#### Safari (iOS)
Settings > Safari > Privacy & Security > Block All Cookies

#### Chrome (Android)
Chrome app > Settings > Site Settings > Cookies

### 6.3 Global Opt-Out Tools

**Industry opt-out tools:**

- **Network Advertising Initiative:** [NAI Opt-Out](https://optout.networkadvertising.org/)
- **Digital Advertising Alliance:** [DAA Opt-Out](https://optout.aboutads.info/)
- **European Interactive Digital Advertising Alliance:** [EDAA Opt-Out](https://www.youronlinechoices.com/)

**Note:** We do not currently use advertising cookies, but these tools can block third-party trackers.

### 6.4 Do Not Track (DNT)

**We honor Do Not Track browser signals.**

When DNT is enabled:
- Analytics cookies are not set (even if previously consented)
- Only essential cookies are used
- Third-party tracking is minimized

**How to enable DNT:**
- **Chrome:** Settings > Privacy > Send "Do Not Track" request
- **Firefox:** Settings > Privacy > Send websites a "Do Not Track" signal
- **Safari:** Preferences > Privacy > Website tracking > Prevent cross-site tracking

## 7. Impact of Disabling Cookies

### 7.1 Essential Cookies

**Blocking essential cookies will:**

- Prevent login (you cannot authenticate)
- Lose security protections (CSRF vulnerability)
- Reset preferences on every visit
- Break core Platform functionality

**We strongly recommend allowing essential cookies.**

### 7.2 Analytics Cookies

**Blocking analytics cookies will:**

- Prevent us from understanding usage patterns
- Limit our ability to fix bugs
- Reduce Platform improvement speed

**Platform will function normally** without analytics cookies.

### 7.3 Performance Cookies

**Blocking performance cookies will:**

- Slightly reduce page load speeds (no CDN optimization)
- Prevent A/B testing (you may miss new features)
- Reduce personalization

**Platform will function normally** without performance cookies.

## 9. Legal Bases for Cookie Use

### 9.1 GDPR (European Union)

**Under GDPR, we rely on:**

| Cookie Type | Legal Basis | Justification |
|-------------|-------------|---------------|
| **Essential** | Legitimate Interest | Necessary for Platform operation (GDPR Art. 6(1)(f)) |
| **Analytics** | Consent | Explicit user consent via cookie banner (GDPR Art. 6(1)(a)) |
| **Performance** | Consent | Explicit user consent via cookie banner (GDPR Art. 6(1)(a)) |

**EU Cookie Directive (ePrivacy Directive 2002/58/EC):**
- Consent required for non-essential cookies
- Users can withdraw consent anytime
- Clear information about cookie purposes

### 9.2 CCPA (California)

**Under CCPA:**

- Cookies constitute "personal information" if they identify a consumer or device
- Right to opt-out of "sale" of personal information (we don't sell data)
- Right to know what information is collected and how it's used

**We provide:**
- Clear disclosure of cookie use (this policy)
- Opt-out mechanisms (cookie banner, browser settings)
- Do Not Sell My Personal Information link (we don't sell data)

### 9.3 Other Jurisdictions

**We comply with cookie laws in:**

- **Canada (PIPEDA):** Consent and transparency requirements
- **Australia (Privacy Act):** Privacy principles for tracking
- **Brazil (LGPD):** Cookie consent and data protection
- **UK (PECR):** Post-Brexit UK cookie regulations

## 10. Special Situations

### 10.1 Children's Privacy (COPPA)

**Our Platform is not directed to children under 13.**

- We do not knowingly set cookies for children under 13
- Parental consent required for users under 13 (future youth features)
- If we discover child cookies, they are deleted immediately

**Parents:** Email privacy@blazesportsintel.com if you believe we've set cookies for your child.

### 10.2 Cross-Border Data Transfers

**Cookies may facilitate data transfers to:**

- United States (our servers)
- European Economic Area (Cloudflare CDN)
- Other countries where service providers operate

**Safeguards:**
- Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) with EU providers
- GDPR-compliant Data Processing Agreements
- Adequate security measures (encryption, access controls)

### 10.3 Automated Decision-Making

**We do not use cookies for automated decision-making that:**

- Produces legal effects
- Significantly affects users
- Requires GDPR Article 22 protections

**Cookie-based personalization** (e.g., showing favorite teams first) is not automated decision-making under GDPR.
